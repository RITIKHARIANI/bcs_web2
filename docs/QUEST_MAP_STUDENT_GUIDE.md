## What is the Quest Map?

The Quest Map transforms your learning journey into an interactive adventure. Each module you complete is like finishing a quest in a game, and you'll earn experience points (XP) and unlock achievements as you progress.

### Key Features

- **Visual Learning Path**: See all modules and how they connect
- **XP System**: Earn points for completing modules
- **Achievements**: Unlock badges for reaching milestones
- **Progress Tracking**: See what you've completed and what's next
- **Prerequisites**: Some modules unlock only after you complete others

## Understanding Modules

### Module Details

When you view a module, you'll see:

**Title**: What the module covers

**Description**: Brief overview of the content

**Difficulty**: How challenging the content is

**XP Reward**: Points you'll earn for completion

- Ensure you're prepared for advanced topics

## Earning XP and Achievements

### Experience Points (XP)

**How to Earn**:
1. Complete a module
2. Click "Mark as Complete" at the bottom
3. Receive XP immediately

**XP Amounts**:
- Quick reads: 50-100 XP
- Standard lessons: 100-200 XP
- In-depth tutorials: 200-500 XP
- Challenges: 300-750 XP
- Boss modules: 500-1,000+ XP

**Leveling Up**:
- XP accumulates across all completed modules
- Each level requires more XP than the last
- Level up to unlock achievements
- Higher levels show your dedication

**View Your XP**:
- Check your profile page
- View the gamification dashboard
- See your current level and progress to next level

## FAQs

**Q: Do I have to complete modules in a specific order?**

A: Not always! Modules without prerequisites can be completed in any order. However, some advanced modules require you to complete prerequisite modules first.

**Q: What happens if I skip a module?**

A: You can skip modules that don't have prerequisites. However, if a module is a prerequisite for something else, you'll need to complete it to unlock those later modules.

**Q: Can I revisit completed modules?**

A: Yes! You can always go back and review content you've already completed. Your progress is saved.

**Q: Will I get XP if I complete a module twice?**

A: No, XP is only awarded the first time you complete a module. However, reviewing content is great for reinforcing learning!

**Q: What if I lose my streak?**

A: Don't worry! Streaks are about building habits, not perfection. Just start a new streak and keep going.

**Q: How do I know which modules are most important?**

A:
- **Boss modules** are major milestones
- **Standard modules** are core content
- **Challenge modules** help you practice
- **Bonus modules** are optional but rewarding

Talk to your instructor if you're unsure about priorities.

**Q: Can I see my friends' progress?**

A: Currently, progress is private. Focus on your own learning journey!

**Q: What's a good learning strategy?**

A:
1. Start with beginner modules (green)
2. Complete prerequisites before moving on
3. Practice with challenge modules
4. Test your knowledge with boss modules
5. Explore bonus content for enrichment
6. Maintain a consistent learning schedule

**Q: I'm stuck on a module. What should I do?**

A:
- Review prerequisite modules
- Check if you need additional background
- Reach out to instructors or classmates
- Take breaks and return with fresh perspective
- Use bonus modules for alternative explanations

**Q: How is my level calculated?**

A: Your level is based on total XP earned. Each level requires progressively more XP:
- Level 1: 0 XP
- Level 2: 100 XP
- Level 3: 400 XP
- Level 4: 900 XP
- And so on... (formula: Level¬≤ √ó 100 XP)

**Q: Do achievements give extra XP?**

A: Yes! Each achievement awards bonus XP on top of the XP you earn from completing modules.
